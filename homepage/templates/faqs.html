FAQ'S

How do I log in?

1. Open browser and navigate to the DawaCare.
2. This automatically loads the DawaCare Login page.
3. Enter user name and password and click login
NB: This only works if the user was added by the admin

How can I add a drug?

1. Go to the navbar and click drug
2. On the drop down menu click “add drug”.
3. Fill in the details accordingly then click add
. 4. To cancel, just click cancel
. 5. You can click reset to reset to default values

How to Edit drug details ?

1. click the drug icon in the navbar and click drug list .
2. Next to the drug you want to edit details, there are 2 buttons click the edit details and edit details accordingly.
3. click save once finished.
